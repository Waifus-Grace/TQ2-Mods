-----------------------------

1. ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Titan Quest II\TQ2\Content\Paks\NightmareDifficulty_P.pak
2. ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Titan Quest II\MultiplayerPreviewVersion\TQ2\Content\Paks\NightmareDifficulty_P.pak
