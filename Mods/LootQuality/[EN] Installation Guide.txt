Drop the folders from the desired version of the mod into the root folder of the game: ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Titan Quest II

Mod contains 2 files. You can check if the mod is installed correctly by checking their presence in the following paths:
1. ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Titan Quest II\TQ2\Content\Paks\LootQuality_x*Mod Version*.pak
2. ...\Steam\steamapps\common\Titan Quest II\MultiplayerPreviewVersion\TQ2\Content\Paks\LootQuality_x*Mod Version*.pak
